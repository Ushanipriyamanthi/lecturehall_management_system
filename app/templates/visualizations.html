About These Visualizations

These visualizations provide insights into lecture hall allocation patterns and utilization rates. They can help identify underutilized resources, optimize scheduling, and improve overall facility management.

Key insights include:

  • Utilization patterns by day of the week
  • Time-of-day usage trends
  • Floor-level utilization comparison
  • Capacity vs. utilization analysis
  • Most and least utilized halls
